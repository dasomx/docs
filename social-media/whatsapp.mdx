### Introduction

Integrating your OKCHAT.AI chatbot with WhatsApp allows for broad customer reach.

### Prerequisites

- WhatsApp Business Account
- Phone number linked to WhatsApp Business API

### Steps

1. **Go to the "Integration" section** in OKCHAT.AI.
2. **Select "WhatsApp"** and click "Connect."
3. **Link your WhatsApp Business number.**
4. **Configure message handling** settings and save.

### Troubleshooting

- Verify phone number is correctly linked.
- Check API status and permissions.

### Best Practices

- Personalize responses for better engagement.
- Use templates for common queries.

### Conclusion

Expand your customer support with WhatsApp integration.
